Features

Everything a CS Practice
Actually Needs

No spreadsheets. No scattered Word files. No missed deadlines. Taxly-CMS replaces the patchwork of tools most compliance professionals use today with one cohesive, intelligent system.

📋
11 Statutory Registers, Ready in Seconds
MGT-1 (Register of Members), MGT-2 (Debentures), DIR-3 (Directors), MBP-1 (Interests), ADT-1 (Auditors), CHG-1 (Charges) and 5 more — all pre-formatted to Companies Act standards, downloadable as letterhead PDFs instantly.
MGT-1 MGT-2 DIR-3 MBP-1 ADT-1 CHG-1 SH-6 DSC
✍️
AI-Assisted Document Drafting
17+ professionally crafted templates — Board Resolutions, AGM Notices, Director's Reports, Audit Reports, Shareholder Lists and more. Auto-fills company, director and auditor details. Period-aware: the {{board_meetings_list}} placeholder fetches every meeting in the selected date range automatically.
Board Resolution AGM Notice Director's Report Audit Report
🏢
Centralised Company Master
One source of truth for every company — CIN, PAN, TAN, GSTIN, authorised capital, ROC details, letterhead logo and registered address. Generate a printable Company Master PDF in one click for client files.
⚠️
Zero-Miss Compliance Alerts
Automated alerts for DIR-3 KYC deadlines, DSC expiry (30-day advance warning), AGM scheduling, charge filing and auditor appointment renewals — ranked by urgency on your dashboard.
🔒
DSC Lifecycle Management
Track every Digital Signature Certificate — issuance, expiry, token type, custody log (with office / with client / returned), and automatic 30-day advance alerts. Never let a DSC lapse unnoticed.
📊
Instant Excel Reports
Download formatted Excel reports for any module filtered by company and date range. Bulk upload 9 modules using pre-formatted templates with colour-coded required fields.
👤
Director & KYC Management
Complete director profiles with DIN, PAN, MCA credentials, email, mobile and address. Track DIR-3 KYC status, last filing and next due date with auto-alerts for the September 30 deadline.
🗓️
Meeting & Minutes Tracker
Schedule and record all Board Meetings, AGMs and EGMs. Smart placeholders auto-fetch every meeting in a financial year — Director's Reports always list all board meetings with no manual BM1, BM2 limits.
👥
Role-Based Access Control
Three clearly defined roles — Super Admin (full control), Manager (data entry and reports) and Staff (view and draft). Secure, audit-ready access with user management and activity logs.
📈
Shareholder Register (MGT-1)
Maintain a current Register of Members with folio numbers, PAN, share class, face value and percentage holding. Auto-populates the MGT-1 register and shareholder list document template instantly.
🔗
Charges & Investment Registers
CHG-1 Charges Register with charge holder, assets charged, creation/satisfaction dates and SRN. Track loans, guarantees, investments and related-party transactions with board resolution dates.
